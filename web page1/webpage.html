WELCOME TO MOUNT ZION COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

ABOUT

Mount Zion College of Engineering, established in 2001, has been active on academic as well as non-academic front since its inception. The college has carved a niche for itself in diverse domains such as Engineering Technology, Information Technology, Business Management, Aviation Industry etc. The campus, spread over 10 acres provides a panoramic view of the valleys in all its splendor. The nearby towns Kozhenchery, Pathanamthitta and Ranni are each at a distance of 10 kms away. The nearest railhead Chengannur is 25 kms away.About 2000 young, energetic, vibrant and budding professionals are pursuing knowledge in their areas of specialization through these programmes in the campus.To provide better practical training, all departments have well equipped labs. Moreover, Mount Zion R & D Lab, an associated research facility of the college allows the students to do the projects at campus itself.Our mission is enabling the young generation to meet the challenges of the futuristic society by equipping them with the technological skills, sustainable values and everlasting ethos.
