ABOUT MBA

Mount Zion School of Business Management, started in 2006 as part of Mount Zion College of Engineering, Kadammanitta is located in a panoramic campus with its entire splendor in Pathanamthitta District, Kerala. It is one of the top B-schools providing quality management education. It offers the coveted MBA (regular) program. It has an excellent track record of academic results, placement services and life skill development programs. The crux of the School lies in the underlying DIVE philosophy that stands 'Discover and Inculcate Value based Enterprise'. DIVE works on experiential learning procedure through Case Studies, Simulated Business games, Industrial Visits, Project study, Mentorship etc. The committed and experienced faculties of the School could produce several rank holders over a decade of its functioning. Being affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University presently, our students have the advantage of Trimester system at par with IIMs and other leading global B-Schools. We offer attractive student support schemes such as free add-on courses, free Industrial visits and out bound adventure trainings etc. Due to the efficient functioning of the placement cell of the School, our alumni are well placed at key positions of leading business firms all over the world.
